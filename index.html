About Us

Salela Projects (PTY) LTD is a dynamic and versatile construction company based in Moletlane, South Africa. We specialize in delivering high-quality projects across the civil engineering and construction sectors. Our services extend to agriculture, water management, energy supply, and hospitality, ensuring an integrated approach to development projects.

We are registered with CIPC, CSD, CIDB, and NHBRC, reflecting our commitment to compliance, transparency, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and innovative construction partner, delivering sustainable solutions that enhance communities and infrastructure.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and high-quality construction and civil engineering services, while contributing positively to the sectors of water, energy, and community development.

Our Core Services

  • Civil Engineering & Construction Projects
  • Water Supply and Waste Management Solutions
  • Energy Infrastructure Development
  • Agriculture and Forestry Project Support
  • Accommodation and Food Service Project Development
